Background

In a world that was shaken by a viral pandemic, humankind shouldn't expose itself to another epidemic, one caused by microorganisms.
  • This experiment used direct current to shock yeast (Schizosaccharomyces Pombe)
  • This research project explored a novel way to treat fungal infection
  • Methods to treat fungal infections are aren't too well developed
  • While the results were not signifcant, this research project gives way to other researchers who plan to explore ways to treat fungal diseases

Rationale
